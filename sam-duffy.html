Sam Duffy

Printer & Poet

About Sam

Sam Duffy is a letterpress printer, poet, and maker of artist books. Their letterpress education began while completing their B.A. in English at UC Berkeley, where they produced their first letterpress project as a collaborative classroom effort. In the years since, they have worked with the CODEX Foundation both as an intern and a collaborator with Peter Koch and the Real Lead Saloon.

Sam's latest project, Fragments of Spring, uses traditional craft to lend credibility to poetic inquiry into transgender identity and belonging. In April 2025, they presented the work at the Berkeley Public Library in response to ongoing violent resistance to queer identities. Sam's next work seeks to confront the rise in anti-trans violence and rhetoric directly, and is currently in pre-production.
