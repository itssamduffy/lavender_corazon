About The Press

Founded by Sam Duffy and Rune de León, Lavender Corazón Press is a micro-press rooted in queer and Latine experience, harnessing the power of letterpress printmaking. Blending the tendencies of fine press printing with the experimental form of the artist book, our work uses the often inaccessible medium of letterpress to surface stories long overlooked.

In an increasingly digital world, we celebrate the tactility of traditional craft, creating printed works that honor both the struggle and beauty of the communities we come from. Lavender Corazón Press also serves as a collaborative imprint through which to publish and co-create with fellow artists and cultural workers in our communities.
