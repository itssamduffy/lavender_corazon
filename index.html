About The Press

Lavender Corazón is a fine press studio dedicated to the art of letterpress printing. We specialize in handcrafted books, artist editions, and unique printed matter that celebrate tactile beauty and thoughtful design. Our work is rooted in a deep appreciation for traditional techniques, quality materials, and the power of the printed word.

We believe in the enduring value of analog craftsmanship in a digital age, creating pieces that are not just read, but experienced. Lavender Corazón also serves as a creative home for our resident artists, Sam Duffy and Rune de Leon, showcasing their individual portfolios and literary explorations.
